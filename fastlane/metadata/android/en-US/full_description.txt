Contact enhances everyday calling and messaging with built-in emergency tools, including silent text-to-911, live video dispatch, real-time location sharing, medical-profile relay, and routing to 988, 311, and 211.

The application provides familiar calls, messages, contacts, a keypad, and recent activity without requiring a separate emergency workflow.

Contact is not a certified emergency service. Its emergency integrations are experimental and educational, and availability depends on device, carrier, location, and public-safety infrastructure.
