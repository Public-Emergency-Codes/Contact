# Code of Conduct

Project participation must remain respectful, constructive, and focused on the work.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate disclosure of personal information, and misuse of emergency or safety-related data are not acceptable. Disagreements should address technical evidence and project impact rather than individuals.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior threatens contributors, users, or the project. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through GitHub.
